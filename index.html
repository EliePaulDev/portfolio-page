About

Learn with Elie, who got into a 6-figure tech job right after college in a Fortune 500 company. I started my journey learning about computer science at City College and CUNY Tech Prep, where I developed a passion for both Web Development and helping others break into tech and succeed in their career. I went from being an immigrant to graduating college and secured my first job as a software engineer at IBM, developing AI-driven web applications. Getting the job before I even graduated. So, I went through the journey of learning and adapting to new environments: a new country, college, and corporation. I've compiled the practical web development knowledge and career insights that will help you build a lasting software engineering career. These fundamentals are designed to be beginner-friendly, making it easy for you to learn and apply. I have helped hundreds before gaining web development skills and breaking into tech. I enjoy giving back and contributing to others' success. I would love to contribute to your success and show you what is possible. Let’s do this. The transformation of your career literally starts with a single decision.
